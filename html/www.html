GRACE (Giving Relief and Care Everywhere)

The online system for charity management that this project will design allows for ease of contribution by donors, increases the participation of local businesses in charitable activities, and ensures that disabled individuals receive the help they deserve. Building up a relationship with local businesses, like hotels and service providers that will be involved in order fulfillment and distribution to people in need.

About us

The Grace platform, Giving Relief and Care Everywhere, is a new solution that will help bridge the gap between donors, providers, and charitable organizations that support people with disabilities. With the platform, it is easy to give charitably in a manner that makes a difference!
